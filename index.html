Video Guidelines

— the dos and the don'ts
✓
Do's
Show the product clearly within the first 2–5 seconds.
Say the full official product name when mentioning.
Only show Urban Decay products in the content.
Keep all content in focus, well-lit, in 4K.
Represent products positively — no smudges, rips, dents.
Zoom in enough to see lips/face & products being applied.
Highlight your favorite benefits in your own words.
Show the dramatic before-and-after transformation.
Be your authentic self — and have fun.
✕
Don'ts
Use distracting, cluttered, or branded backgrounds.
Feature landmarks or trademarked locations.
Wear branded or revealing clothing.
Use false lashes (All Nighter content).
Use filters, heavy retouching, or color effects.
Include personal logos, watermarks, GIFs, or text overlays.
Shoot from side angles — keep it straight on, vertical.
Use unapproved music — always music-check.
Show any third-party IP — labels, logos, posters, artwork.

Common Questions

— before you ask, check here
How do I get my affiliate link or Spark Code? +

Your Spark Code authorization and unique affiliate links are issued through TikTok Shop Seller Center once your participation is confirmed. Message your campaign manager on Lark or Slack to request them — they'll be issued to you within 24 hours of campaign confirmation. If your samples are already delivered and you haven\'t received codes, message your CM directly on Lark.

When do commissions pay out? +

TikTok Shop pays affiliate commissions on a monthly cycle, typically 30-45 days after the order is marked delivered (not just shipped). Use the Commission Calculator above to project earnings — your actual payout reflects fulfilled, non-returned orders only.

What happens if my video doesn\'t pass the compliance review? +

Your campaign manager will flag the issue (most common: missing verbal disclosure, prohibited claim, or wrong product name) and request a reshoot or edit. Address it within the requested window so the video can be posted in the campaign\'s active dates. #UrbanDecayPartner in caption and a clear verbal disclosure are the two most common fails — get those right first.

Can I use my own music? +

No. Only tracks from TikTok\'s Commercial Music Library are approved for branded affiliate content. Trending songs, copyrighted tracks, and your own original audio require additional clearance and will usually fail review. Music-check before posting.

Can I show competitor products in my video? +

No. Frame only shows Urban Decay products. Direct comparisons (calling out specific competitor names) are not allowed. Indirect comparisons ("this lasts longer than my old setting spray") are okay as long as no specific brand is named or shown.

Do I have to use every hook angle in the brief? +

No — the hooks are starting points. Pick one or two that fit your style and audience. The brief gives you four angles per product specifically so you have options. Save your favorites with the ★ button and reference them later.

How do I submit my draft for pre-approval? +

Message your campaign manager directly on Lark or Slack with the unlisted TikTok draft URL. Pre-approval is encouraged but not required — if you\'re confident the video meets the standards in this brief, you can post directly.

Who do I contact if I have questions? +

Your assigned campaign manager on Lark or Slack is the first point of contact. They can answer questions about sample status, compliance edge cases, performance data, and Spark Code issues. For urgent or escalation items, use the ↗ Share button to send the relevant brief section directly to them.
